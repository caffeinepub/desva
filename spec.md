# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Remove all product images from the Cute Accessories page so product cards display text-only content.

**Planned changes:**
- Remove all AI-generated earring images from product cards in `CuteAccessoriesPage.tsx`
- Remove the thumbnail switcher/gallery component from the Cute Accessories page
- Ensure no broken image elements or empty image containers remain
- Adjust product card layout to display cleanly without image space, retaining name, description, price, tags, and add-to-cart functionality

**User-visible outcome:** The Cute Accessories page shows product cards with only text-based content (name, description, pricing, tags, add-to-cart) and no images or empty image placeholders.
